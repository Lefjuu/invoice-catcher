# Invoice Catcher

> Stop digging through your inbox for invoices. Let the app do it for you.

## What it does

Invoice Catcher connects to your email account, finds messages that contain invoices (as PDF attachments or links), and lets you save them to a folder on your computer with a single click. No more manual searching, downloading attachments one by one, or losing track of where you put them.

## Why you'll like it

- **Saves time** – Scans hundreds of emails in seconds and shows only the ones that actually contain invoices.
- **Works with any email** – Gmail, Outlook, your company mail, or any provider that supports IMAP.
- **Keeps things tidy** – Choose a folder and move all your invoice PDFs there in one go.
- **Remembers everything** – Your email settings, folder choice, and even the list of found invoices stay saved locally between sessions.
- **Preview before you save** – Look at the PDF or the email content right inside the app.
- **Stays on your computer** – Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Your email password never leaves your machine.

## How to get it

Head over to the [Releases page](../../releases/latest) and download the installer for Windows, macOS, or Linux.

Just run the file and you're good to go – no extra software required.
